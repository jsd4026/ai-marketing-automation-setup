Vireo Tech  
Usage Rules & Restrictions  

February 2026  

Vireo Tech  
San Francisco  

---

## Purpose  
These rules ensure the Vireo brand remains consistent, premium, understated, and unmistakable. Any deviation risks diluting the quiet intelligence that defines us.  

All creative, marketing, legal, and third-party usage must follow these guidelines exactly. No exceptions without written approval from brand stewardship.

---

## 1. Logo Usage  

### Primary Logo  
- The 'V' symbol (fluid arc, tilted left, razor edges with soft ends)  
- Versions: Silver on black / Black on silver / White on obsidian / Teal pulse variant (glow at joint only for digital boot/animation)  

### Clear Space & Minimum Size  
- Minimum clear space: 1.5 × height of the 'V' on all sides  
- Minimum size: 24 px digital / 0.25 in print (never smaller)  
- Never crop, rotate, animate (except approved pulse), or add effects  

### Forbidden Modifications  
- No stretching, compressing, skewing, or recoloring  
- No outlines, drop shadows, gradients (except approved teal pulse)  
- No placing on busy/patterned backgrounds  
- No combining with other logos/symbols unless in official co-branding (pre-approved)  

### Lock-up (Wordmark)  
- When used with "Vireo Tech" or "Vireo": centered below, Vireo Sans Regular, 60% of logo height spacing  
- Preferred: Logo alone for most hero usage  

---

## 2. Trademark & Naming Rules  

### Registered Marks  
- Vireo®  
- Vireo Tech®  
- Nexus One™  
- Nexus™ (in context of the device)  

### Correct Usage  
- First use in copy: Include ® or ™ as appropriate  
- Subsequent uses: No symbol required  
- Always capitalize: Vireo, Nexus One (never "nexus one" or "VIREO")  
- Never pluralize, verbify, or abbreviate (no "Nexuses", no "Vireo-ing")  

### Forbidden Naming  
- No nicknames ("the V", "Nexus phone") in official copy  
- No "Vireo iPhone", "Vireo Apple alternative", or direct competitor references  

---

## 3. Color & Typography Restrictions  

### Color Palette  
- Obsidian: #333333 — primary background/text  
- Titanium: #FFFFFF — primary accent/background  
- Teal Pulse: #00A0FA — single-use accent only (never dominant, never fill)  
- No other colors permitted without approval  

### Typography  
- Font family: Vireo Sans only (no substitutes)  
- Weights: Light (300), Regular (400) — no bold, italic except for subtle emphasis  
- No system fonts (Helvetica, San Francisco, Arial) as fallback in official assets  

---

## 4. Imagery & Visual Restrictions  

- Product shots: Follow Approved Product Photography & Renders Guidelines exactly  
- No people, hands, or lifestyle scenes in primary/hero imagery (exception: ultra-minimal silhouette, pre-approved)  
- No stock photography  
- No memes, humor, or pop-culture references  
- No heavy filters, vignettes, grain, or vintage effects  

---

## 5. Tone & Copy Restrictions  

- Follow Tone of Voice & Editorial Guidelines strictly  
- Forbidden words/phrases: revolutionary, best, ultimate, game-changer, insane, epic, wow, incredible, next-gen, disrupt  
- No exclamation marks in primary messaging  
- No questions in hero copy  
- No bullet lists in hero/lead messaging  
- Emojis: Forbidden in official copy (social exceptions rare and approved)  

---

## 6. Packaging & Physical Asset Rules  

- Box: White magnetic lid, matte finish, no gloss  
- Interior: Black velvet tray only  
- No plastic clamshell, foam peanuts, or excess inserts  
- No printed manual — AI onboarding only ("Tell me how it works")  

---

## 7. Digital & Third-Party Usage  

- Website/social: White/obsidian dominant, teal accent once per frame  
- Ads: Must use approved templates (no custom designs)  
- Partner/co-marketing: Pre-approval required; logo size ≥ competitor logos  
- Merchandise/press kits: Only through official channels  

---

Any usage outside these rules must be submitted for review.
