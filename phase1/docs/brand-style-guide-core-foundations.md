Vireo Tech Brand Style Guide
 
Vireo Tech Established: two thousand twenty-six San Francisco, Earth.
 
Logo
Single silver 'V'. Fluid arc. Tilted left. Razor edges, soft ends. Use on matte black. Or black on matte silver. Lock-up: 1:1.3 aspect. Padding—no less than one point five times height. Never stretch.
Color Palette
•	Obsidian: #333333
•	Titanium: #FFFFFF
•	Teal Pulse: #00A0FA — Use once. Sparks life.
Textures None. Flat world. Unless light hits metal—then let reflection dance.

 
 
Primary Font
Vireo Sans. Clean. Rounded terminals. Weights: Regular (four hundred), Light (three hundred).
Hierarchy
•	Headlines: Vireo Sans Regular. Thirty pixels. All caps. Track: two hundred.
•	Body: Vireo Sans Light. Sixteen pixels. Line-height: one point five.
•	Captions: Eight pixels. Gray. Fade.
No exclamation marks. No bold. No lists.
 
Camera: ARRI seven-five-zero-three. ISO low. F two point eight. One thousandth shutter.
Shot Rules:
•	Product floats. No stand.
•	Backgrounds: white, black, fog.
•	Depth: blur the edge of reality.
•	No faces. No hands. Phone moves alone.
Frame ratio: nine-by-sixteen for reels. One-by-one for stills.
 
Short. Still. Breath.
Examples:
•	Think. It listens.
•	No app. Just now.
•	Hold it. World folds.
No questions. No hype. Let silence finish the sentence.
 
One truth: Intelligence isn't in the code. It's in what you never say.
